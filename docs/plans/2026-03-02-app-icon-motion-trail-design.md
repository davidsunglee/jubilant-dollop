# App Icon Redesign: Bird + Motion Trail

## Overview

Update the existing app icon to convey action and speed. The bird scales up for more presence and gains a horizontal motion trail — three fading ellipses trailing to the left — that communicates velocity without adding competing geometry.

## Canvas

- **Base size:** 1024×1024px (iOS universal)
- macOS sizes generated by scaling down from the 1024px base (unchanged)

## Background

- Linear gradient, top to bottom — unchanged
- Top: `#D4EEFF` (soft sky blue)
- Bottom: `#FDE8D0` (warm peachy horizon)

## Bird (Avian)

- **Scale:** ~75% of canvas (≈768px diameter, up from ~60–65%)
- **Position:** Slightly left of center — approximately 40px left of canvas center — to give the motion trail room on the left without crowding the beak on the right
- All other properties unchanged:
  - Body: yellow circle with thin orange stroke
  - Belly: cream/light yellow oval overlay
  - Eye: white circle with black pupil, upper-right of body
  - Beak: orange triangle pointing right
  - Wing: gold ellipse angled slightly up (mid-flap pose)
  - Tail: 2 small feathered ellipses fanning left
  - Subtle drop shadow (black ~5% opacity, slight y-offset)

## Motion Trail

Three horizontally-elongated ellipses trailing off to the left of the bird, centered on the bird's horizontal midline. No stroke — filled shapes only, fading out leftward.

| Trail | Size (w × h) | Fill | Opacity | Offset from previous |
|-------|-------------|------|---------|----------------------|
| 1 (closest) | 85 × 55px | Bird yellow | 40% | Just off left edge of bird |
| 2 (middle)  | 60 × 40px | Bird yellow | 25% | ~80px further left |
| 3 (farthest)| 40 × 26px | Bird yellow | 12% | ~70px further left |

The elongated horizontal shape (roughly 1.5:1 width-to-height ratio) reads as a streak rather than a ghost copy.

## Intentionally Omitted

- No pipes, clouds, or ground
- No text or title
- No speed lines or comic-book effects

## Implementation

- Update the Swift CoreGraphics script (`scripts/`) that generated the original icon
- Re-export all PNG sizes from the updated 1024px base
- Replace assets in `Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/`
